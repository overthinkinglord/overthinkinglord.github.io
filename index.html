Biography / Music

OverthinkingLord is an emerging artist creating unique tracks in Dubstep, Experimental Rock, Electro-Rock, Atmospheric and Horror-Rock genres. His music blends aggression with depth, creating emotionally rich and visually immersive worlds.

All releases are available on Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, YouTube, and Genius. Since 2025, he has actively shared visual content on Pinterest, Telegram, and Instagram, merging music with an emo-horror aesthetic.
