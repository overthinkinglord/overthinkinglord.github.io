Biography

Overthinkinglord is an independent dubstep, electro-rock, and horror-rock music artist, known for atmospheric and experimental music. Since 2025, he has been producing tracks that blend heavy basslines, cinematic soundscapes, and haunting melodies, captivating listeners worldwide.

His major releases, including Phosphorus, Lithium, and Cementary of Darkness 2, are available on Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, and YouTube.

Overthinkinglord’s music combines aggressive energy with eerie, cinematic atmospheres, establishing him as a rising independent artist in the electronic and horror-rock music scene. Discover his unique sound and explore his full discography online.
